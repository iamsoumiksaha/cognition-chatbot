# ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# DIGITAL MARKETING CONCEPTS
# ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
what is digital marketing ||| Digital marketing means promoting your business online through SEO, social media, websites, and ads to reach more customers.
what is seo ||| SEO (Search Engine Optimization) improves your website to rank higher on Google, bringing free organic traffic.
what is ppc ||| PPC (Pay Per Click) means you pay only when someone clicks your ad. Google Ads and Facebook Ads use PPC.
what is content marketing ||| Content marketing creates valuable blogs, videos, and posts to attract and engage potential customers.
what is email marketing ||| Email marketing sends promotional or informational emails to your subscribers to nurture leads and retain customers.
what is lead generation ||| Lead generation attracts potential customers and collects their contact information for sales follow-up.
what is conversion rate ||| Conversion rate is the percentage of visitors who take a desired action like buying, calling, or signing up.
what is bounce rate ||| Bounce rate is the percentage of visitors who leave your website without taking any action.
what is ctr ||| CTR (Click Through Rate) is the percentage of people who click your ad out of those who see it.
what is cpc ||| CPC (Cost Per Click) is the amount you pay each time someone clicks your ad.
what is cpm ||| CPM (Cost Per Mille) is the cost to show your ad to 1,000 people.
what is roi ||| ROI (Return on Investment) measures profit earned compared to marketing spend. Higher ROI means better performance.
what is roas ||| ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) shows revenue earned per rupee spent on ads. 4x ROAS means ₹4 earned per ₹1 spent.
what is organic traffic ||| Organic traffic is visitors who find your website through search engines without paid ads. SEO drives organic traffic.
what is paid traffic ||| Paid traffic comes from advertising like Google Ads and Facebook Ads.
what is a keyword ||| A keyword is a word or phrase people type into search engines. Targeting the right keywords is key to SEO.
what is a backlink ||| A backlink is a link from another website to yours. Quality backlinks improve your Google rankings.
what is domain authority ||| Domain Authority (DA) predicts how well a website will rank on search engines. Higher DA = better ranking potential.
what is google my business ||| Google My Business (Google Business Profile) is a free listing that shows your business on Google Maps and Search.
what is local seo ||| Local SEO helps your business appear in Google searches for services near a specific location.
what is remarketing ||| Remarketing shows ads to people who previously visited your website to bring them back and convert them.
what is a landing page ||| A landing page is a focused web page designed to convert ad visitors into leads or customers.
what is a call to action ||| A CTA (Call to Action) prompts visitors to act: "Call Now", "Book Free Consultation", "Get Quote".
what is a sales funnel ||| A sales funnel shows the customer journey: Awareness → Interest → Decision → Action.
what is brand awareness ||| Brand awareness measures how well people recognize and remember your business.
what is brand identity ||| Brand identity includes your logo, colors, typography, and visual style that represent your business.
what is target audience ||| Target audience is the specific group of people most likely to buy your product or service.
what is google analytics ||| Google Analytics tracks website visitors, their behavior, traffic sources, and conversions.
what is google search console ||| Google Search Console shows how your website performs in Google searches, including rankings and errors.
what is facebook pixel ||| Facebook Pixel tracks website actions to improve Facebook ad targeting and measure conversions.
what is a b testing ||| A/B testing compares two versions of an ad or page to find which performs better.
what is core web vitals ||| Core Web Vitals are Google's performance metrics: loading speed, interactivity, and visual stability.
what is responsive design ||| Responsive design makes websites look and work well on all screen sizes including mobile and desktop.
what is ssl ||| SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encrypts website data showing the padlock icon. Google favors HTTPS sites.
what is marketing automation ||| Marketing automation uses software to automate repetitive tasks like email campaigns and social posts.
what is crm ||| CRM (Customer Relationship Management) software helps manage your customer data and sales pipeline.
what is influencer marketing ||| Influencer marketing uses popular social media figures to promote your brand to their audience.
what is social proof ||| Social proof includes reviews, testimonials, and case studies that build trust in your business.
seo vs ppc ||| SEO builds long-term free organic traffic. PPC gives immediate paid results. Both work best together.
google ads vs facebook ads ||| Google Ads target active searchers. Facebook Ads build awareness by audience. We recommend combining both.
organic vs paid marketing ||| Organic (SEO, content) is sustainable and free long-term but slower. Paid ads bring fast results but need ongoing budget.
